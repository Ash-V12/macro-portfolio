Macro Market Strategy Portfolio

Author: Arshia Verma | Date: June 2025

Project Overview

This self-led project explores how inflation and interest rate cycles from 2020 to 2025 influenced U.S. equities and bond markets. Using synthetic Excel data, I analyzed monthly S&P 500 trends, Treasury yield movements, and their correlation, proposing a barbell investment strategy aligned with institutional macro thinking. This was done as part of my ongoing learning and passion for understanding financial markets and macroeconomics.

Key Insights

  • Moderate inverse correlation between 10Y Treasury yields and S&P 500 returns.
  • Equity returns compressed during rate hike cycles, especially 2022–2024.
  • Investment thesis favors defensives and long-duration bonds.
